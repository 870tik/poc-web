    • Our Mission

    In order to realize a sustainable society that takes the environment and human rights into consideration, we must pay attention not only to the colors and designs of our products but also to the materials and methods used in them. I intend to create a story in which people who take the products that have been made can naturally improve the world. Also, by understanding the background of the material, I hope that you can feel the meaning of the clothes you are wearing.

    Unlike chemical fibers, cotton, which many people wear every day, is a familiar clothing material that can be grown in a natural environment. However, many people still do not know who makes it and where it affects the environment.

    This led to the creation of POC with the aim of supporting the farmers' desire to grow cotton without using agrichemicals, by looking at each farmer's face and facing the cotton fields from planting seeds to harvesting.

    POC, which is grown using the same cultivation method as organic cotton, is a program to shift to an organic world through the cooperation of both the producer and the buyer.

    By making meaningful choices, consumers can improve the environment and the lives of producers.Our mission at the POC Secretariat is to design a business model that will support these farmers so that they can continue to grow cotton with peace of mind and reach as many consumers as possible.
