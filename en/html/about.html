    • Pre-organic cotton program

    "We are working to increase 1% organic cotton by Pre Organic Cotton."
    "Organic cotton" has recently become a common sight on the streets.Even so, the production of organic cotton is only about 1% of the world's total cotton production.
    Through our "Pre-Organic Cotton (POC)" activities, we are working to expand the cultivation of organic cotton, which is kind to both people and the earth, by connecting farmers in India with consumers around the world.

    About pre-organic cotton

    Describes in detail "Pre-Organic Cotton (POC)," cotton that has been grown without agricultural chemicals for three years until it is certified as organic cotton.

    POC is organic cotton that was harvested before being certified as organic cotton and grown without using any agricultural chemicals.
    Typically, a three-year transitional cultivation period is required to obtain organic certification. During the three years leading up to the switch to organic cotton, many farmers face various challenges, such as "reduced yields" and "resulting in reduced income," and many farmers give up organic cultivation.

    We named that three-year period of organic cotton (called 'transition period cotton') as' pre-organic cotton 'and launched it as a project to support the shift towards organic society.
    Through the Pre-Organic Cotton (POC) Program, we are promoting the distribution of cotton harvested during the transition to organic cultivation, and supporting more farmers in the transition to organic cotton by increasing demand for cotton during the transition period.
